Linux is an amazing operating system, I prefer to use it for development because it's open source, and centralized repositories are the way to go when it comes to installing software, rather than have to Google for installers, and hope to God that your version of Windows likes whatever software you're attempting to install. My current distribution of choice is Linxu Mint 17, but I have also used Ubuntu (destop/server), Fedora, Gentoo, and Debian.
